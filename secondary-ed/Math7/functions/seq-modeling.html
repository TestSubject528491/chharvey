Lesson Sequence Components

Before Phase

Brief overview of what it means to be a sequence, basic examples of sequences, "Can you guess what the next number/shape/picture would be?"

During Phase

Pose a sequence and ask students to find the 100th number/shape/picture in the sequence.

Lesson Analysis Components

Justification

This project allows students to take everyday objects and relate them to mathematics. They would have to be creative and would be able to incorporate their cultures and personal experiences to the project. Additionally, this project is equitable because students can use anything, instead of having to rely on technology that they might not have access to.
