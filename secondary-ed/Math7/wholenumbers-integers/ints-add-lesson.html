Lesson 1.4: Adding Integers

SOL
7.3a The student will model addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division of integers.
7.3b The student will add, subtract, multiply, and divide integers.
Timeframe
Week I.3
Time required
3 days
Lesson created

Lesson updated

Preparation

Objectives

  • The student will model addition of integers (with the same sign and different signs) using two-colored counters, Algeblocks™, and a number line.
  • The student will compute the sum of two integers, with the same sign or different signs.

Essential Questions

  • What is an integer?
  • How do I add integers when they have the same sign?
  • How do I add integers when they have different signs?

Vocabulary

sum
the answer to an addition problem
zero pair
a pair of units that, when added, make 0
