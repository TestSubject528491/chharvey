Chosen Colors

These colors ...

  • Aperture White The purity of Aperture Science in all its glory. This dazzling brilliance of bluish light seen on the portalable panels coated with Conversion Gel makes the background emit an eerie glow, like the full moon.

    • <body> background

  • Aperture Black The polar opposite of Aperture White. Representing the true dark nature of the Enrichment Center, this is the color of the dark panels that inhibit portals. A hint of orange is infinitesimally noticeable. What better way to compliment the background color than the color of normal text?

    • <body> text
    • .block-up.active, .block-dn.active background

  • Atlas Blue Short and stout, Atlas is one of the two robots used for testing. Untouched hyperlinks are dedicated to his digital iris. This is also the color of Repulsion Gel and forward-moving Excursion Funnels.

    • .up text & border
    • .block-up text & border

  • P-body Orange Tall and wiry, P-body is one of the two robots used for testing. Hovered hyperlinks are dedicated to his (or her?) digital iris. This is also the color of Propulsion Gel and backward-moving Excursion Funnels.

    • .dn text & border
    • .block-dn text & border

  • Bridges of Light Hard Light Bridges are literally massless, yet they can sustain infinite weight, as Chell can attest to.

    • .block-up background

  • Asbestos The forgotten underground test chambers are ridden with this harmful substance. Or so Mr. Johnson says.

    • .block-dn background

  • Lake Superior This Great Lake borders Ontonagon, MI, the location of the Aperture Science Enrichment Center. A perfect fit for superior headings on a page.

    • main heading text

  • Facility Abyss Representing the endless depths of the subterranean Enrichment Center, this colors sub-headings of a page.

    • sub-heading text

  • Cave's Caves ...

    • sub-heading text in .block-dn

  • Are You Still There? Four words that you never want to hear without a portal gun. Just as the iris of a Sentry Turret, this color is briefly seen, providing a subtle accent on the site.

    • .up.active, .dn.active text & border
    • accent border

  • HER Eye This color alone will scare the prokaryotic-microorganism-infested-fecal-matter out of you. See where you can find it. You monster.

    • .block-up.active, .block-dn.active text & border

  • Neurotoxin Long ago an accident resulted in the unleashing of this deadly vapor and the murdering of the entire human staff of Aperture Laboratories. Some say it could happen again, but this time on the test subjects. You can escape via a special link with this color.

    • .sp-up text
    • .new / <ins> text

  • Vilify ...

    • .sp-dn text
    • .old / <del> text

  • Heart of a Companion Believed to be sentient by some, and alive by others, the Weighted Companion Cube has been said to provide advice and display emotions such as brevity, care, and love. It is even believed to hum opera when near a friend. A perfect color for helpful elements on a Web page.

    • .help border
