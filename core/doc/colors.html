    Portal Color Scheme

    Aperture White

    The purity of Aperture Science in all its glory. This dazzling brilliance of bluish light seen on the portalable panels coated with Conversion Gel makes the background emit an eerie glow, like the full moon.

    • body background

    Aperture Black

    The polar opposite of Aperture White. Representing the true dark nature of the Enrichment Center, this is the color of the dark panels that inhibit portals. A hint of orange is infinitesimally noticeable. What better way to compliment the background color than the color of normal text?

    • body text

    Atlas

    Short and stout, Atlas is one of the two robots used for testing. Untouched hyperlinks are dedicated to his digital iris.

    • link text
    • button border

    P-body

    Tall and wiry, P-body is one of the two robots used for testing. Hovered hyperlinks are dedicated to his (or her?) digital iris.

    • hover link text
    • active button border

    Repulsion Gel

    Good news, you’re not part of the control group; you get the gel. Bad news, it does not like the human skeleton. Turns out you might just break every bone in your legs anyway.

    Propulsion Gel

    “Forwarding” the cause of science.

    Deploying

    The one word you never want to hear without a portal gun. Just as the iris of a Sentry Turret, this color is briefly seen, providing a subtle accent on the site.

    • postal, figure, block quote border
    • table border
    • footnote border

    HER Eye

    This color alone will scare the prokaryotic-microorganism-infested-fecal-matter out of you. See where you can find it. You monster.

    • active link text

    Lake Superior

    This Great Lake borders Ontonagon, MI, the location of the Aperture Science Enrichment Center. A perfect fit for superior headings on a page.

    • major heading text
    • drop caps

    Cave’s Caves

    Cave’s underground salt mines were the ideal venue for early test chambers. This color is for “minor” headings of a page.

    • minor heading text

    Facility Abyss

    This color can be seen in the endless depths of the subterranean Enrichment Center.

    • major subheading text

    Asbestos

    The forgotten underground test chambers are ridden with this harmful substance. Or so Mr. Johnson says.

    • minor subheading text

    Neurotoxin

    Long ago an “accident” resulted in the unleashing of this deadly vapor and the murdering of the entire human staff of Aperture Laboratories. Some say it could happen again, but this time on the test subjects…

    • table background base

    Heart of a Companion

    Believed to be sentient by some and alive by others, the Weighted Companion Cube has been said to provide advice and display emotions such as brevity, care, and love. It is even believed to hum opera when near a friend. A perfect color for helpful elements on a Web page.

    • helpful elements

    Bridges of Light

    Hard Light Bridges are literally massless, yet they can sustain infinite weight, as Chell can attest to.

    Vilify

    You’ve got sucker’s luck. Have you given up? Does it feel like a trial? Does it trouble your mind the way you trouble mine?

    Label Color Scheme

    Each of the five label colors are equidistant in hue. The border colors are the base hues, the text colors are a shade, and the background colors are a tint. Both the shade and the tint form a Golden proportion with the base hue.
