Relations

Ordered Pairs

Ordered Pairs. Formal and necessary.

\begin{align} (a,b) &:= \{\{a\},b\}\\ (a,b,c) &:= \bigl((a,b),c\bigr) = \Bigl\{\bigl\{(a,b)\bigr\},c\Bigr\} = \Bigl\{\bigl\{\bigl\{\{a\},b\bigr\}\bigr\},c\Bigr\}\\ (a,b,c,d) &:= \bigl((a,b,c),d\bigr) = \dots = \biggl\{\Bigl\{\Bigl\{\bigl\{\bigl\{\{a\},b\bigr\}\bigr\},c\Bigr\}\Bigr\},d\biggr\} \end{align}
\begin{align} (a,b) &:= \{a,\{b\}\}\\ (a,b,c) &:= \bigl((a,b),c\bigr) = \bigl\{(a,b), \{c\}\bigr\} = \Bigl\{\bigl\{a,\{b\}\bigr\},\{c\}\Bigr\}\\ (a,b,c,d) &:= \bigl((a,b,c),d\bigr) = \dots = \biggl\{\Bigl\{\bigl\{a,\{b\}\bigr\},\{c\}\Bigr\},\{d\}\biggr\} \end{align}

Two ordered pairs are equal if and only if their corresponding components are equal.

Tuples. Informal and not part of the system.

\begin{align} \varnothing &:= \{\}\\ \langle\rangle &:= \{\varnothing,\varnothing\} &&= \{\varnothing\}\\ \langle a\rangle &:= \bigl\{\langle\rangle,a\bigr\} &&= \bigl\{\{\varnothing\}, a\bigr\}\\ \langle a,b\rangle &:= \bigl\{\langle a\rangle,b\bigr\} &&= \Bigl\{\bigl\{\{\varnothing\}, a\bigr\}, b\Bigr\}\\ \langle a,b,c\rangle &:= \bigl\{\langle a,b\rangle,c\bigr\} &&= \biggl\{\Bigl\{\bigl\{\{\varnothing\}, a\bigr\}, b\Bigr\}, c\biggr\}\\ \langle a,b,c,d\rangle &:= \bigl\{\langle a,b,c\rangle,d\bigr\} &&= \Biggl\{\biggl\{\Bigl\{\bigl\{\{\varnothing\}, a\bigr\}, b\Bigr\}, c\biggr\}, d\Biggr\} \end{align}
reflexive:
(\forall a\in s) (a\sim a)
irreflexive:
(\exists a\in s) (a\not\sim a)
antireflexive:
(\forall a\in s) (a\not\sim a)
(implies irreflexive)
symmetric:
(\forall a,b\in s) (a\sim b \implies b\sim a)
asymmetric:
(\exists a,b\in s) (a\sim b \land b\not\sim a)
antisymmetric:
(\forall a,b\in s) (a\sim b \implies b\not\sim a)
(implies asymmetric)
something else:
(\forall a,b\in s)(a\sim b \land b\sim a \implies a=b)
transitive:
(\forall a,b,c\in s) (a\sim b \land b\sim c \implies a\sim c)
intransitive:
(\exists a,b,c\in s) (a\sim b \land b\sim c \land a\not\sim c)
antitransitive:
(\forall a,b,c\in s) (a\sim b \land b\sim c \implies a\not\sim c)
comparable (total):
(\forall a,b\in s) (a\sim b \lor b\sim a)
uncomparable:
(\exists a,b\in s) (a\not\sim b \land b\not\sim a)
anticomparable: (iff the relation is empty)
(\forall a,b\in s) (a\not\sim b \land b\not\sim a)
defined:
(\forall a\in s) (\exists b\in s) (a\sim b)
undefined:
(\exists a\in s) (\forall b\in s) (a\not\sim b)
well-defined:
(\forall a,b,c\in s) (a\sim b \land a\sim c \implies b=c)
(\forall a,b,c,d\in s) (a\sim b \land d\sim c \land a=d, \implies, b=c)
not well-defined:
(\exists a,b,c\in s) (a\sim b \land a\sim c \land b\not=c)
(\exists a,b,c,d\in s) (a\sim b \land d\sim c \land a=d, \land, b\not=c)
injective:
(\forall a,b,c\in s) (a\sim b \land d\sim b \implies a=d)
(\forall a,b,c,d\in s) (a\sim b \land d\sim c \land b=c, \implies, a=d)
surjective:
(\forall b\in s) (\exists a\in s) (a\sim b)
