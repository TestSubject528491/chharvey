     About Me

    Hey there! I’m Chris Harvey, and I love math, music, working out, coding, and hanging out with my dog, Axel. Fairfax, VA is my hometown. I graduated from Fairfax High School, and earned degrees in Mathematics and Education from Virginia Tech. Someday, I plan to go back and finish my Music degree. Right now I live in Charlottesville, VA, enemy territory.

    I have two years of experience teaching math (Algebra II and then 7th Grade Math). While those experiences were valuable and educational, I’ve decided to take a break from my teaching career. I want to get paid doing something that has always been just a hobby—developing for the Web.

    Employers: Check out my résumé. I did a pretty good job describing my skill set and experience, but I feel the source code speaks for itself.
