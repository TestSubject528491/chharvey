    Math is my strength.

    Math is just something that comes to me easily, and if I’m having trouble, it usually won’t take long for me to learn. I find myself always thinking about math in everyday situations. Right now I'm a math teacher. I try my best to use my advanced mathematical knowledge to my advantage when teaching secondary-education level material. But not only do I teach students; sometimes they teach me too. I’ve learned so much, both educationally and mathematically, as a teacher. Apart from math I also have interests in computer science and Web development, science fiction, physics, and astronomy.
